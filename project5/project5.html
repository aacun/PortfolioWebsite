    • Sundry - An Assortment of Projects

    These two posters below are personal projects I created for experimentation and pushing my creative boundaries. They feature vibrant colors, dynamic gradients, and textured grain, blending bold visual elements to express artistic freedom.


    FASO Exhibit Boards

    These six exhibition boards were created for Notre Dame's Filipino American Student Org's (FASO) annual show, "Fiestang." They highlight traditional Filipino dances and performances, capturing the vibrancy and cultural significance of each piece in a visually engaging format. The boards were designed to celebrate and showcase the rich heritage of the Filipino community through captivating visuals.


    Strike Magazine ND

    For Issue 6 of Strike magazine’s Notre Dame chapter, I created six spreads themed around "washed up" as part of the larger "tempest" concept. Collaborating with a team, we explored stormy, evocative visuals to bring this narrative to life while maintaining a cohesive design across the issue.
