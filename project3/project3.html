    • Concert Poster Design Series

    This project features a series of three posters I designed for artists I enjoy, incorporating some images I took during their live concerts. Each image was edited in Lightroom to highlight the energy and emotion of each performances, blending my love for music and design into a cohesive visual experience.


    Audience: Music Lovers, Concert-Goers

    Central Themes Concert Experiences, Poster Advertisements:

    Goal: Create designs that align with each artist's aesthetic and visual identity
